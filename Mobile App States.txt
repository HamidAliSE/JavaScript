Android Applications States (4)
01. Active: Can Interact, Can Perform Tasks, In Foreground
02. Inactive: Can't Interact, Paused, In Background
03. Background: Can't Interact, Running, In Background, Not Visible
04. Suspended: Neither Foreground Nor Background, No Resource Use, Completely Shut Down

iOS Applications States (5)
01. Not Running / Terminated: Not Launched, Not Running, Terminated by User or OS, Neither Foreground Nor Background
02. Active: Running, In Foreground, Receive Events
03. Inactive: Running, In Foreground, Not Receive Events
04. Background: Running, In Background
05. Suspended: Not Running, In Background, In Memory, No Resource Use
